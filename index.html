Life exists in motion

Cellulata conducts dynamic single cell analysis at scale

Cellulata® understands how thousands of individual cells move, activate, kill and survive

TIMING™ evaluates immune cell activity as a function of time with visual AI to understand which candidates to move into clinical trials, to understand response, and to maintain consistent manufacturing.

Visually evaluate how your cells perform and interact over time

Time-lapse Imaging Microscopy in Nanowell Grids (TIMING) applies neural network based detection to evaluate thousands of individual cell-cell interactions

Characterize motility, contact and cell death for thousands of cells

Evaluate thousands of cells in parallel to characterize motility, cellular interactions, cell death and survival, and biomolecule secretions

Identify and retrieve cells of interest for downstream profiling

The TIMING platform allows Cellulata to identify and retrieve cells of interest to perform additional profiling such as single‑cell RNA sequencing and protein expression.

Integrate motion and performance with multi‑omic profiling

Cellulata links data and insight on kinetics, interactions and other functional measures with information from analysis modalities such as single-cell RNA sequencing and flow cytometry to provide a comprehensive understanding of cellular function, state and phenotype.
